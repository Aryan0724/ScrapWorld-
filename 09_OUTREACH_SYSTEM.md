# Overview

The Outreach System converts intelligence into conversations.

SCRAPE WORLD does not send generic outreach.

Every outreach message must be based on real business intelligence.

The system uses:

* Business Data
* Website Intelligence
* Competitor Intelligence
* AI Insights
* CRM History

To generate highly personalized outreach.
