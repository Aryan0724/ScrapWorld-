# Overview

The AI Engine is the intelligence layer of SCRAPE WORLD.

Scraping alone has low value.

Intelligence creates value.

The purpose of the AI Engine is to transform raw business data into actionable sales opportunities.

---

# AI Mission

Convert:

```text
Raw Business Data
```

Into:

```text
Business Intelligence
```

And then into:

```text
Sales Opportunities
```

---

# Core Principle

The AI should answer:

1. What is wrong with this business?
2. How serious is the problem?
3. What opportunities exist?
4. What services can solve it?
5. What should we say to the prospect?
6. Why would they buy?
