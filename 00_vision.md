# scrapeworld

## Vision

scrapeworld is an AI-powered Lead Intelligence Operating System designed for agencies.

Most lead generation tools stop at scraping.

scrapeworld discovers businesses, enriches them with data from multiple sources, audits their digital presence, analyzes competitors, identifies opportunities, generates sales intelligence, and provides a complete CRM workflow.

The platform transforms raw business listings into actionable revenue opportunities.

The objective is not to collect leads.

The objective is to identify businesses that are most likely to buy services.

---

# Core Philosophy

Traditional workflow:

Lead → Outreach

Antigravity workflow:

Lead → Intelligence → Opportunity → Outreach → CRM → Revenue

The system must answer six questions:

1. Who should I contact?
2. Why should I contact them?
3. What problem do they have?
4. How serious is the problem?
5. What are competitors doing better?
6. What should I say to close them?
